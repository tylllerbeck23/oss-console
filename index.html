What it does

A command console for finding fish.

Live AI vision

Watch the camera feed; the AI flags surfacing fish, boils & bait in real time with on-screen targeting boxes.

Automated search

Draw an area, pick a pattern, and export a DJI Pilot 2 mission (.kmz) your drone flies autonomously.

FAQ

Good to know.

What drone do I need?

It’s built around the DJI Mavic 3T (thermal/IR), which flies the exported Pilot 2 missions and streams its camera. Other DJI Enterprise / Dock aircraft work too, and any HLS/WebRTC/RTMP feed or webcam for the AI.

Is the AI really detecting fish?

Yes — in the live app the model runs on your actual feed and drops GPS-tagged contacts. The website demo is a choreographed visualization of that overlay (a browser can’t read a YouTube video’s pixels). Detection sharpens as you label your local species.

Subscription or one-time?

Fishing is a one-time license with free updates. The SAR edition is licensed per team, annually, since it’s used operationally by organizations.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Install it to your phone/tablet home screen and it runs offline — your data stays on the device. Optional Google Sheets sync keeps the crew in one logbook.

How do I get my key after buying?

Checkout is handled by our payment provider; your license key is emailed to you on purchase. Enter it once and the app activates on that device.
