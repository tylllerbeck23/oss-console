📖 Link Your DJI Mavic 3T

The Mavic 3T is Enterprise-tier, so it runs DJI Pilot 2 — it can fly OSS missions automatically and stream its thermal/visible camera into this console.

A
Live camera feed
  1. On the CONNECT tab, open “DJI RTMP → gateway”, set your gateway IP, and start the MediaMTX command on a laptop on the same network.
  2. In DJI Pilot 2 → ··· → Live Streaming → RTMP, paste rtmp://<gateway>:1935/oss and Start.
  3. Paste the HLS/WebRTC URL back into Camera Feed → Connect Feed. Toggle the payload to IR/thermal in Pilot 2 for heat-contrast spotting.
B
Automated search flight
  1. PLAN FLIGHT → set your vessel → pick a pattern → Generate → Simulate Flight to confirm it fits one battery.
  2. Export DJI Mission (.kmz). Move the file onto the RC (USB-C / microSD / Drive). The RC is Android.
  3. In Pilot 2 → Waypoint → Import → open the .kmz. Confirm takeoff point & altitude, then fly — the M3T runs the grid and returns home on its own.
C
Spot & log fish
  1. Watch the feed on OPERATE; when you see a school, tap Mark Fish on Map (or the AI feed drops it in) — you get an alert and it logs to your hotspot history + Google Sheet.
  2. Review past hotspots any time on the LOGBOOK tab and the map heatmap.
⚠ Fly under 400 ft AGL, keep visual line of sight (or proper BVLOS authorization), and check airspace (B4UFLY / LAANC) before launch. You are the pilot in command.
